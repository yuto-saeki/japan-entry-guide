# Japan PMF Sprint

A 12-week, fixed-price process to answer one question: **Does your product fit Japan?**

---

## Scope

6 deliverables. No ambiguity.

1. **Market landscape** — Size, segments, and competitive dynamics in your category
2. **Voice of customer interviews** — 8–12 interviews with Japanese buyers or end users
3. **Makuake or LP test** — A live market test to measure real demand
4. **Channel economics** — Unit economics for the most viable distribution channels
5. **GTM recommendation** — A prioritized path to market, with rationale
6. **Go / No-Go decision** — A clear recommendation, not a hedged consultant report
